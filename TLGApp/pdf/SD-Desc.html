Description

Overview Align+© Structure Recognises that every company is different, and there is no set formula for determining the right design or structure for your organisation. However, an organisation must be clear on its overall 'strategic value driver'. So, we begin by asking: 'what are you really offering to your clients and what must you be exceptional at'? From this simple review, effective and aligned organisational design can begin.
Align+© Structure: What does it do? Align+ Structure asks you to evaluate where the focus of your strategic intent needs to be, by asking about the three main value drivers of strategic success:
  • Operational excellence
  • Customer excellence
  • Product excellence

The overall comparison between perceptions at a leadership team level will allow discussions to determine if the correct focus has been applied to the current organisation. More detailed analysis can then follow through facilitated workshops and organisation design discussions.
Potential intervention: What you could do as a consequence
  • Leadership organisation design workshop
    • Determine single strategy -v- portfolio mix and implications
    • Strategy Execution Model (SEM©) alignment
  • Operating model review
    • Evolving the operating model that is needed to execute the organisational strategy effectively
  • Organisation design workshop/s
    • Based on an agreed organisation design process
  • Review of reward consistency
  • Capability review
    • Organisation fit / gaps
    • Capabilities -v- structure
