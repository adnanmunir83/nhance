Description

Overview Align+© Leadership Is based on the certainty that the leadership team needs to have the capabilities required to make sure the strategy can be executed. As the competitive environment shifts and changes over time, requirements for success will also change.

Align+ Leadership will help your organisation consider the capabilities that leaders need today and in the future in order to be able to deliver the full impact required of them as leaders.
Align+© Leadership: What does it do? Leadership capability can be broadly divided into 4 Dimensions:
  • Performance Leadership: enhancing the performance of individuals and teams
  • Strategic Leadership: anticipating customers' needs through a thorough understanding of the industrial domain within which the business operates
  • System Leadership: taking a 'whole system' leadership role to optimise system performance
  • Inclusive Leadership: nurturing a culture to deliver the strategy of the business

Completion will help an individual leader:
- understand their profile of strengths and weaknesses
- see instantly what the most urgent areas of attention are for them

The organisation can then:
- decide when and where to focus attention and budgets for development,education and team-building.
- decide, based on their profiles, which people will be most effective leading the business towards the successful execution of the business strategy.
- see the levels of confidence in capabilities at a team, function or organisation level and compare with our Align+© Leadership index, against the most successful organisations.
