Overview Align+© Brexit Recognises that Brexit is a complex issue that will potentially have a range of consequences for Businesses in the UK and in Europe. However, no two organisations are exactly the same and therefore a ‘one size fits’ all approach cannot work effectively. This diagnostic helps leaders to de-construct complex issues into simpler, more tangible pieces, and to identify and address the issues that really matter..

Align+ Brexit has been designed to sift through the mass of possible implications and help you to focus on those that really matter.
Align+© Brexit: What does it do? Align+© Brexithas 5 sections, each looking at different elements that can determine how Brexit can affect your business.
  • Threats (or opportunities) relating to Revenu
  • Threats (or opportunities) relating to Exchange Rate Movements
  • Threats (or opportunities) relating to the organisation Cost Structure
  • Threats (or opportunities) relating to Availability of Skills
  • Threats from potential Non-Tariff Barriers (both in post Brexit Europe as well as new markets that might need to be explored to replace revenue lost in Europe)

    • These questions are based on The Leadership Gallery research into the issues most likely to affect businesses post Brexit.

    In order to de-construct complex issues, Align+© Brexit specifically asks, against each issue, the following to be graded, separately:
    • The size or significance of the Impact on the organisation
    • The Probability of this happening
    • The Confidence Level of the business in being able to deal with the outcome.
    Our proprietary software combines the implications of impact, probability and confidence, to produce an Index as well sharply differentiated scores against each issue.
    Align+© Brexit is typically administered, at the outset, to the Leadership Team of an organisation. Subsequently, depending on the organisations needs, it can be administered to large cross-sections of the organisation.
    Potential intervention: What you could do as a consequence
    • Viability review based on possible tariff outcomes.
    • Capability review - looking at capability and infrastructure requirements for potential new markets
    • Leadership review potentially using the Align+© Leadership diagnostic.
    • Alternative supply chain review to identify alternatives
    • Subsidiary options and organisation governance/risk review potentially using Align+© Risk
    • Organisation structure design review potentially using Align+ Structure.
