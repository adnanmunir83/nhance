Align+ Strategy: What does it do?

Instead of offering packaged solutions out of a box, Align+ Strategy helps identify with precision, the specific areas, where action is required in order to enable successful execution of strategy.

3 major headings:

Robust :
How good are the assumptions we have made about markets, competitors, capabilities, costs, regulations, technology?
Resilient :
Is the strategy still viable if a couple of assumptions go wrong?
Ready :
Especially where the strategy involves a major change of direction, are our structures, processes, decision making mechanisms, metrics, reward systems future facing?

Each section is divided into 2 parts :

Looking outwards :
factors and issues that are outside the organisation's control, but could influence it's future.
Looking inwards :
factors and issues directly within the company's control.
One of it's great strengths is that there are just 3 questions in each section, which makes it easier for users to respond with full concentration.
The questions are simple and at the same time, deep. They force the respondents to think deeply about the issues touched by each question.
Responses are analysed in our server, and instantly generate scores and powerful visual analyutics.
These help to identify:
  • The issues that are seen unanimously as areas requiring attention.
  • More importantly, it highlights the variances of perception within the Leadership Team (or across the organisation) on the same issues. These almost always represent the faultlines and misalignments within organisations, that can derail successful execution of strategy.

    This analysis becomes the fulcrum for consulting interventions. The impact of the interventions are heightened by combining Align+ Strategy responses with 1:1 interviews with key respondents. Between the two, a rich context is built for a workshop environment where management can agree specific actions, timelines and accountabilities.
