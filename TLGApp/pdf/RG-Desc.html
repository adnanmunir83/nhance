Description

Overview Align+© Risk Align+ Risk helps Boards and top management to firstly, recognise risk in all it's forms, and secondly, to confirm whether governance systems are in place to deal with these.

Based on the responses to Align+© Risk's clear, sharp questions, the diagnostic is able to highlight the critical areas of potential risk that need to be addressed specifically.
Align+© Risk: What does it do? Align+© Risk is divided into 2 parts, alongside each other: one addressing risks, the other confirming whether governance systems are, in fact, in place to deal with these risks.

Align+© Risk addresses 5 kinds of risk:

- Technical Risks

- Regulatory Risks

- Regulatory Risks

- Financial Risks

- Reputational Risks



Align+© Risk asks respondents to indicate how confident they are that the business can manage the risks identified, and how good the governance systems to deal with the risk are.



The Leadership Gallery's proprietary algorithms automatically generate a 'Risk Index', which allows the business to see how concerned or reassured they should be.



The Risk Index allows management to drill down into those specific aspects that are driving the scores, and focus on the issues that urgently need to be addressed. For example, a high risk perception accompanied by a poor governance system rating, should automatically raise red flags.



Align+© Risk diagnostic is typically administered, at the outset, to the Board or the Leadership Team of an organisation. Subsequently, depending on the organisation's needs, it can be administered to large cross-sections of the organisation.
Potential intervention: What you could do as a consequence
  • Digital disruption review / review of business model / operational plan
  • Crisis management review - implementation of new crisis management approach
  • Strategic planning review ,incorporating strategy review - Using Align+© Strategy, Align+© Process and Align+© Leadership to check if the strategic planning process explicitly addresses risks associated with technical obsolescence, patent expiry or capability gaps
  • Functional review of internal audit function: Does it explicitly address business risks or is it restricted to compliance issues? Using Align+© Board to check how effective Board governance is.
  • Facilitated workshops to establish confidence that issues that can affect the business' overall reputation (and therefore it's valuation) have crisis management systems and training in place?
