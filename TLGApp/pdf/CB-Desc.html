Description

Overview Align+© Culture Is based on the understanding that an organisations culture is the result or consequence of how the business is structured, the processes and systems within it, the rules, governance and policies by which it operates. It therefore follows, that the organisation culture must be consistent with the strategy of the business. If there is a mis-alignment between the culture and the way the organisation needs to work, then the strategy will not be executed effectively.

Align+ Culture, helps leaders within the business to plan where / how to change and develop the organisations culture, so that there is a greater chance for the successful execution of the business strategy.
Align+© Culture: What does it do? Align+ Culture diagnostic helps leaders and employees describe organisation culture and behaviour, tests this against the culture / behaviour required to support the strategy, and so allows the organisation to decide where interventions may be required.
You will be asked to review the culture of your organisation against 4 specific dimensions::

  • The bosses - where the bosses make the decisions
  • The functions - where functions have power
  • The expertise - where expertise is valued
  • 'Me' - where individuals can assume power and decision making

Align+© Culture asks participants to evaluate the 'as-is' culture and separately the 'to-be' culture. These two different perspectives will help the organisation to determine how well the current culture is supporting the successful execution of the business strategy and what needs to change.
Potential intervention: What you could do as a consequence
  • Leadership workshops to define:
    • - Purpose
    - Vision
    - Values
    - Audit, risk and evaluation
    - The remuneration committee
  • Strategy alignment workshop/s
  • Organisational process evaluation and simplification
  • HR process alignment workshops and planning
    • - Talent planning, succession and recruitment
    - Performance management
    - Reward
    - Organisation Development
  • Engagement and culture alignment programmes
  • Organisation re-design
