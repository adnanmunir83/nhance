Overview Align+© Engagement Recognises that employee engagement occurs when the right conditions exist / are established, for all members of an organisation to want to deliver their best endeavours every day. It can be seen when employees are committed to their organisation's purpose, vision, values and goals. An organisation will be able to see clearly when employees are motivated to contribute to the organisations' success.

Employee engagement is based on the relationship that the employee has with their direct manager/supervisor, how they understand their own role, the level of personal accountability they believe they have and also their clarity about the organisations purpose and strategy.

Align+ Engagement allows an organisation to measure itself, highlighting gaps or mis-alignments and as a result, for employee engagement to be nurtured and dramatically increased.
Align+© Engagement: What does it do? Align+ Engagement diagnostic helps managers/leaders and employees evaluate separately their own views of engagement against 4 specific dimensions :

  • Establishing role clarity: are employees clear about their roles?
  • Managing relationships: how well do we help nurture the relationships between managers and employees
  • Allowing autonomy and control : are employees clear about what they are accountable for and where they can make decisions?
  • Helping employees to understand the purpose and strategy : how clear are all employees about our core purpose and our strategy?

The 'gaps' between leader/manager and employee perceptions will give a very clear view of the challenges that need to be addressed. The overall score -v- the engagement index will provide a focus for the organisation.
